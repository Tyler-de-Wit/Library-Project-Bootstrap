Student Portal

Instructions

It may take up to 24-48 hours after completing your enrolment for your account to become active in all our online systems.

First you MUST change your default password in either Microsoft 365 (student email) or on a Student PC

If you have already changed your password on a college PC or with a Library staff member, then use the new password you have created.

Login To The Student Portal

Student Portal

You must have set-up you Microsoft 365 Multi-factor Authentication before you attempt to log onto the portal.

Select 'Log on using Office 365' and log in using your student email and your student password.

DO NOT "Log on using your details"

Complete Any Tasks Due

From here you can access your results, re-enrol and change your details. But first, check if your 'My Tasks' icon has a number in red. If it does, click on it to find out what tasks require your attention.

Password Expiry

Your TAFE password expires every 6 months - you MUST change it before it expires or you will be locked out of all our online systems.

You can change your password on a Student PC by using CTRL+ALT+DELETE and choosing Change a Password.

Or via the settings in Microsoft 365 (student email) - click on the settings cog in the top right hand corner of your Microsoft 365 account and choose change your password.

Password Requirements

  • Must be a minimum of 14 characters and can be any combination of letters or numbers.
  • Cannot reuse a password you have already used
  • Cannot include your first or surname or your date of birth

If you are having problems changing your password please contact your Campus Library

For more information about the student portal can be found by opening the file below, Navigating the Student Portal.
